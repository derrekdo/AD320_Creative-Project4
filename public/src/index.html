Monsters

Monster Hunter is video game franchise developed and published by Capcom. The first game Monster hunter was released in 2004 for the Playstation 2. The game has evolved over the years, and is currently in the 5th generation with the most recent game being Monster Hunter Rise.

In monster hunter the player takes on the role of a "hunter" and takes on various quests to hunt or study monsters that may prove a threat to the village or ecosystem. With a huge catalog of armors and weapons, the hunter can improve their power based on gear they craft. Every monster come in different colors and sizes, with some being more dangerous than others.
